    About Bharat Electronics Ltd.

    Bharat Electronics Ltd., incorporated in the year 1954, is a Large Cap company (having a market cap of Rs 42059.21 Crore) operating in Electronics sector.

    Bharat Electronics Ltd. key Products/Revenue Segments include Electronic Components & Equipments which contributed Rs 6700.74 Crore to Sales Value (87.44 % of Total Sales), Sale of services which contributed Rs 820.90 Crore to Sales Value (10.71 % of Total Sales), Other Operating Revenue which contributed Rs 114.54 Crore to Sales Value (1.49 % of Total Sales), Export Incentives which contributed Rs 14.27 Crore to Sales Value (0.18 % of Total Sales), Rent which contributed Rs 6.51 Crore to Sales Value (0.08 % of Total Sales) and Scrap which contributed Rs 5.75 Crore to Sales Value (0.07 % of Total Sales)for the year ending 31-Mar-2016.

    For the quarter ended 30-06-2017, the company has reported a Standalone sales of Rs 1694.55 Crore, down -55.82 % from last quarter Sales of Rs 3835.20 Crore and up 98.36 % from last year same quarter Sales of Rs 854.30 Crore Company has reported net profit after tax of Rs 125.32 Crore in latest quarter.

    The company’s top management includes Dr.Ajit T Kalghatgi, Dr.Bhaskar Ramamurthi, Dr.R K Shevgaonkar, Mr.Girish Kumar, Mr.M V Gowtama, Mr.Nataraj Krishnappa, Mr.Ramchander N Bagdalkar, Mr.Sharad Sanghi, Mrs.Anandi Ramalingam, Mrs.Kusum Singh, Mrs.Usha Mathur. Company has Badari, Madhusudhan & Srinivasan as its auditoRs As on 30-06-2017, the company has a total of 2,233,627,930 shares outstanding.
