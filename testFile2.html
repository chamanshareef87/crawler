    About Oil And Natural Gas Corporation Ltd.

    Oil And Natural Gas Corporation Ltd., incorporated in the year 1993, is a Large Cap company (having a market cap of Rs 207770.08 Crore) operating in Petroleum sector.

    Oil And Natural Gas Corporation Ltd. key Products/Revenue Segments include Oil Crude which contributed Rs 51896.33 Crore to Sales Value (66.05 % of Total Sales), Gas Natural which contributed Rs 18266.37 Crore to Sales Value (23.24 % of Total Sales), Liquefied Petroleum Gas which contributed Rs 3519.44 Crore to Sales Value (4.47 % of Total Sales), Naphtha which contributed Rs 3051.06 Crore to Sales Value (3.88 % of Total Sales), C2/C3 (Ethane/Propane) which contributed Rs 894.53 Crore to Sales Value (1.13 % of Total Sales), Subsidy which contributed Rs 290.16 Crore to Sales Value (0.36 % of Total Sales), Superior Kerosene Oil which contributed Rs 211.78 Crore to Sales Value (0.26 % of Total Sales), Other Operating Revenue which contributed Rs 167.91 Crore to Sales Value (0.21 % of Total Sales), Electricity which contributed Rs 65.17 Crore to Sales Value (0.08 % of Total Sales), Processing Charges which contributed Rs 53.30 Crore to Sales Value (0.06 % of Total Sales), Propane which contributed Rs 49.58 Crore to Sales Value (0.06 % of Total Sales), Low Sulphur Stock which contributed Rs 41.18 Crore to Sales Value (0.05 % of Total Sales), HSD which contributed Rs 40.56 Crore to Sales Value (0.05 % of Total Sales), Mineral Oil which contributed Rs 7.46 Crore to Sales Value (0.00 % of Total Sales), High Speed Diesel (Trading) which contributed Rs 5.72 Crore to Sales Value (0.00 % of Total Sales), Motor Spirit which contributed Rs 4.37 Crore to Sales Value (0.00 % of Total Sales), Others which contributed Rs .26 Crore to Sales Value (0.00 % of Total Sales)for the year ending 31-Mar-2016.

    For the quarter ended 30-06-2017, the company has reported a Standalone sales of Rs 19073.54 Crore, down -12.16 % from last quarter Sales of Rs 21714.02 Crore and up 7.94 % from last year same quarter Sales of Rs 17670.37 Crore Company has reported net profit after tax of Rs 3884.73 Crore in latest quarter.

    The company’s top management includes Dr.Santrupt Misra, Mr.A K Dwivedi, Mr.A K Srinivasan, Mr.Ajai Malhotra, Mr.Amar Nath, Mr.D D Misra, Mr.D K Sarraf, Mr.Deepak Sethi, Mr.K M Padmanabhan, Mr.Rajiv Bansal, Mr.Shashi Shanker, Mr.Sumit Bose, Mr.T K Sengupta, Mr.V P Mahawar, Mr.Vivek Mallya, Prof.Shireesh B Kedare. Company has PKF Sridhar & Santhanam LLP as its auditoRs As on 30-06-2017, the company has a total of 12,833,235,180 shares outstanding.
